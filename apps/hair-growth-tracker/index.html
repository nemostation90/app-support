Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

GrowLine is designed as a local-first private photo journal. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed by the app and by third-party services used for ads and purchases.

Information You Provide

  • Progress photos you choose to capture or import.
  • Routine items, routine completion logs, focus areas, goals, and notes you enter.

This content is stored locally on your device. We do not operate an account system or upload your hair photos to our own server.

Camera and Photo Library

The app asks for camera or photo library access only when you choose to take or import a progress photo. You can manage permissions in iOS Settings.

In-App Purchases

Pro purchases and subscription status are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may process payment, purchase history, and account information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policy.

Advertising

The free version may show Google AdMob interstitial ads. AdMob may process identifiers, advertising data, product interaction data, diagnostics, and related data for advertising and measurement. The app may request App Tracking Transparency permission if personalized advertising is enabled. You can decline tracking and still use the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal photos or notes. We do not use your photos for medical diagnosis, model training, or advertising.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your in-app photos, routines, logs, and notes remain on your device until you delete the app or remove entries. You can also delete exported files wherever you saved or shared them.

Medical Disclaimer

GrowLine is for personal organization and visual progress journaling only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or prevention of any condition. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for hair loss, scalp concerns, medication, or treatment questions.
