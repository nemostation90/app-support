Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add items to my inventory?

Tap the + button on the Inventory tab. You can add items manually, scan a barcode, scan a receipt, or use voice input (premium features).

How does the shopping list work?

When items run low (below your set threshold), they appear in the Shopping List tab. Tap "Generate from Low Stock" or pull down to refresh. Check off items when you've bought them — the quantity is automatically added back to your inventory.

What's included in the free version?

Free users can track up to 50 items with manual entry. Premium unlocks unlimited items, barcode scanning, receipt OCR, voice input, budget tracking, export, and removes ads.

How does household sharing work?

Premium users can create a household and share a unique code with family members. Members join using the code and get access to the shared inventory and premium features. All changes sync in real-time via iCloud.

Can I change the app language?

Yes! Go to Settings → Language and choose from 21 supported languages. The app switches instantly without restarting.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → Manage Subscription, which opens your Apple ID subscription settings. You can cancel there at any time.

How do I delete all my data?

Go to Settings → Delete All My Data. This permanently removes all items, categories, shopping lists, and purchase records from your device.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

StockPal stores your household inventory data (item names, quantities, expiry dates, photos, and categories) locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. When CloudKit sync is enabled, this data is stored in your private iCloud account.

2. Advertising

StockPal uses Google AdMob to display ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads through the App Tracking Transparency prompt shown when you first use the app, or in your device's Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

3. Subscriptions

StockPal uses Apple's StoreKit 2 to manage in-app subscriptions. Payment is handled entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not process or store payment information.

4. Data Storage

All inventory data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account. We do not operate any servers that store your data. Household sharing uses Apple's CloudKit shared database, which is managed entirely by Apple's infrastructure.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising (free tier only)
  • Open Food Facts — barcode product lookup (public API, no account required)
  • Apple CloudKit — data sync and household sharing (your iCloud account)
  • Apple Vision / Speech — on-device receipt OCR and voice recognition (no data sent to servers)

6. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, health data, or browsing history. We do not sell any data to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

StockPal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Delete All My Data. This removes all inventory items, categories, shopping lists, and purchase records from your device. Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
