Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does GarageLog calculate fuel economy? It uses your odometer and fill-up volume, and correctly handles full tanks, partial fills, and the occasional missed fill-up so your MPG / L/100km is accurate.
  • Can I use miles and gallons — or kilometers and liters? Yes. Units and currency are fully configurable app-wide, and you can override them per vehicle.
  • Does it support electric vehicles? Yes. GarageLog tracks kWh and charging costs for EVs alongside gas, diesel, and hybrid vehicles.
  • How do reminders work? Set a reminder by mileage, by date, or both — GarageLog warns you whichever comes first and can predict the due date from how you actually drive.
  • Where is my data stored? On your device. GarageLog is local-first — there are no accounts and your records are not uploaded to our servers. You can export everything to CSV or PDF at any time.
  • What does GarageLog Pro include? Unlimited vehicles, advanced insights and anomaly alerts, smart predictive reminders, CSV & PDF export, and no ads. Available as a one-time Lifetime unlock or a monthly / yearly plan.
  • How do I restore my purchase? Open Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. Pro is tied to your Apple ID.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

GarageLog is designed as a local-first, private logbook. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed by the app and by the third-party services used for ads and purchases.

Information You Provide

  • Vehicle details (name, make, model, year, and an optional photo or VIN you choose to add).
  • Fuel, service, and expense records, reminders, notes, and any receipt photos you add.

This content is stored locally on your device. We do not operate an account system and we do not upload your vehicle records, notes, or photos to our own server.

Camera and Photo Library

The app asks for camera or photo library access only when you choose to add a vehicle photo or attach a receipt. You can manage these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Notifications

With your permission, GarageLog schedules local notifications to remind you about upcoming maintenance and expiring documents. These reminders are generated on your device.

In-App Purchases

GarageLog Pro is available as a one-time Lifetime purchase or as an auto-renewing monthly or yearly subscription, processed by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may process payment, purchase history, and account information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policy. We never see your payment details.

Advertising

The free version shows Google AdMob banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may process identifiers, advertising data, product interaction data, diagnostics, and related data for advertising and measurement. The app requests App Tracking Transparency permission before any tracking; you can decline and still use every feature. Upgrading to GarageLog Pro removes all ads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your vehicle records, notes, or photos. We do not use your data for model training.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your vehicles, logs, reminders, notes, and photos remain on your device until you delete them or remove the app. You can delete individual records or entire vehicles at any time from within the app.
