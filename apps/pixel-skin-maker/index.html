Make your own skin, pixel by pixel.

How to use your skin in Minecraft

Export the PNG to Photos or Files, open Minecraft, go to the Dressing Room, edit your character, open Classic Skins, choose Import, pick your PNG, and select Classic or Slim to match. Custom (non-Marketplace) skins may only be visible to other players if "Only Allow Trusted Skins" is turned off — that is a Minecraft setting, not ours.
