FAQ

What is RoutinePix for?

Parents create picture-based routines for daily steps like getting dressed, packing a bag, brushing teeth, chores, and bedtime.

How do I start?

Use Onboarding or the Routines tab to choose a template, then customize steps with icons, timers, or your own photos.

Where are photos stored?

Photo cards are stored in app-private storage on your device unless you export or share a file.

How do I delete data?

Open Settings → Data Controls → Delete All Data to remove children, routines, cards, photos, templates, and sessions from this device.

How do purchases work?

RoutinePix Pro is handled by Apple StoreKit. Use Restore Purchases if you reinstall or switch devices.

Why do I see ads?

The free plan may show AdMob banner and interstitial ads. RoutinePix Pro removes ads.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

RoutinePix is a local-first family organization app. It does not require an account and does not operate a RoutinePix backend for routine or photo storage.

Information stored on your device

  • Child nicknames or profile labels you enter.
  • Routines, routine steps, timers, cards, templates, and completion sessions.
  • Photos you choose for routine cards, stored in app-private local storage.
  • Lightweight settings such as onboarding completion and selected child.

Third-party services

  • Apple StoreKit: used for subscriptions, lifetime purchase, purchase validation, and Restore Purchases. Apple handles payments and payment details.
  • Google AdMob: used to show ads on the free plan. AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising data, product interaction, and diagnostics for advertising and measurement. RoutinePix requests Apple App Tracking Transparency permission before personalized advertising identifiers are used. If you decline, ads may still appear but should be limited/non-personalized where supported.

Data we do not collect

RoutinePix does not ask for an account, password, birthday, address, contacts, health data, or precise location. We do not sell RoutinePix routine or photo data.

Deleting your data

You can delete all local RoutinePix data in the app from Settings → Data Controls → Delete All Data. Exported PDFs or files shared with other apps are controlled by those destinations.

Children

RoutinePix is intended for parents or guardians to create routines for family use. Children should not create accounts because the app has no account system. If you believe personal information was sent to us by email, contact us and we will delete it where possible.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to certain data processing. For privacy requests, email pals_green.1m@icloud.com.
