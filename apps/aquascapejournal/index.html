Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does dosing work? Enter your tank's water volume and target parameters, and the app suggests fertilizer dosing as an informational husbandry estimate. Always verify with a test kit and a drop checker, and tune by observation.
  • Where is my data stored? Your tanks, readings, dosing plans, photos and logs stay on your device. There is no account and nothing is uploaded to our servers.
  • How do I delete all my data? Open Settings inside the app, scroll to "Privacy & data" and tap "Delete all data" to permanently remove everything from your device.
  • What does Aquascape Journal Pro include? A one-time Lifetime purchase removes ads and unlocks unlimited tanks, the full salt & product database, advanced & custom parameters, multi-parameter charts & insights, the plant deficiency assistant, and CSV & PDF export.
  • How do I restore my purchase? Open Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchase. Pro is tied to your Apple ID.
  • Can I change the app language? Yes. Aquascape Journal is localized into 20 languages and follows your iOS language setting.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Aquascape Journal is designed as a local-first, private aquarium logbook. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed by the app and by the third-party services used for ads and purchases.

Information You Provide

  • Tank profiles, water parameter readings, dosing plans, water-change logs, plants, livestock, maintenance tasks and notes you enter.
  • Scape photos you choose to capture or import into the journal.

This content is stored locally on your device. We do not operate an account system and we do not upload your photos or logs to our own server.

Camera and Photo Library

The app asks for camera or photo library access only when you choose to take or import a journal photo. You can manage these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

In-App Purchases

Aquascape Journal Pro is a one-time, non-consumable purchase processed by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may process payment, purchase history, and account information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policy. We never see your payment details.

Advertising

The free version shows Google AdMob banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may process identifiers, advertising data, product interaction data, diagnostics, and related data for advertising and measurement. The app requests App Tracking Transparency permission before any tracking; you can decline and still use every feature. Purchasing Aquascape Journal Pro removes all ads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your photos or journal data for model training or for advertising.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your in-app tanks, readings, plans, photos, and logs remain on your device until you delete them or remove the app. You can delete individual items at any time, and you can erase everything at once from Settings → Privacy & data → Delete all data.

Husbandry Disclaimer

Aquascape Journal provides informational husbandry estimates for planted aquariums based on the water volume and values you enter. Dosing figures are not veterinary or medical advice — verify with a test kit and a drop checker, and tune by observation. Store fertilizer salts sealed and away from children and pets.
