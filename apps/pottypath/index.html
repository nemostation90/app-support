Privacy Policy

Potty Path is built privacy-first — everything you enter stays on your device.

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Overview

Potty Path is built privacy-first. All data you enter stays entirely on your device — we have no servers, no accounts, and no cloud backend. We never see, transmit, or store your data, and we do not share it with any third party.

Data We Collect

All of the following is stored locally on your device only, using Apple's SwiftData framework:

Child profile (optional, parent-entered):

  • Child's first name or nickname (optional)
  • Gender
  • Age band
  • Potty-training onboarding answers: whether you've tried before, observed readiness signs, preferred approach, focus, setting, current challenge, motivator, caregivers involved, target timeframe, and any concerns
  • Optional night-time window preference

Potty log entries (parent-entered):

  • Timestamp of each event
  • Type of event and outcome
  • Location
  • Whether the child was prompted
  • Resistance level
  • Optional free-text note

Milestone entries you record in the app.

None of this data is ever transmitted to any external server. There is no analytics, no advertising, no third-party data sharing, and no behavioural profiling.

On-Device Guidance

Potty Path's tips, routines, and coaching content are bundled inside the app and rendered entirely on your device. The app does not connect to any AI service, server, or third-party API for guidance. No text you enter ever leaves your device.

App Content

All articles, routines, troubleshooting cards, and tips are bundled inside the app. No content is downloaded from external servers during use.

In-App Purchases

Purchases (monthly subscription, yearly subscription, and lifetime access) are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Potty Path never handles or stores any payment information. Your purchase status is verified locally via Apple's StoreKit framework.

Notifications

Potty Path sends local notifications only — gentle potty-time reminders and progress nudges. These are scheduled on-device by iOS and are never sent through any external notification service. You can disable them at any time in your iOS Settings.

Analytics & Tracking

Potty Path contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, no attribution tools, and no tracking of any kind. We do not use Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, RevenueCat, AppsFlyer, or any equivalent service. Our PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy file declares NSPrivacyTracking = false with no tracking domains.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Potty Path is designed to be operated by a parent or guardian on behalf of their child. The app does not knowingly collect data directly from children. All input, purchases, and settings are managed by the adult who installed the app. Because all data is stored locally on the device, it is fully controlled by the device owner — no child data is ever transmitted off-device or shared with us or any third party.

iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled in your iOS Settings, your device's local data (including Potty Path data) may be included in your iCloud backup. This is controlled entirely by your iOS settings and is not initiated by Potty Path. The app itself does not sync to iCloud or any other cloud service.

Data Deletion

To permanently delete all Potty Path data, delete the app from your device. Because we hold no data on external servers, this is complete and irreversible.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will note them in the App Store release notes. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

What Potty Path Is

Potty Path is an educational parenting app that helps families navigate potty training. The app provides:

  • A readiness check based on widely-used pediatric readiness signals
  • A personalised, age-band-appropriate plan and daily routines
  • Gentle, parent-led troubleshooting tips
  • An on-device potty log to track patterns and progress

All guidance and content runs entirely on your device. Potty Path does not connect to any server, AI service, or analytics provider.
