Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I make a pattern? Open a preset from the gallery or pick a pattern type, then drag the sliders. Everything animates live — tap Shuffle for instant inspiration.
  • Why do the loops play seamlessly? Loopart renders each animation deterministically so the last frame flows perfectly back into the first. What you preview is exactly what exports — no visible seam.
  • What can I export? Looping MP4 video and animated GIF, in square and vertical aspect ratios sized for social feeds.
  • What does Loopart Pro include? HD & Ultra-resolution export, no watermark, every premium palette, and an ad-free experience — all as a single one-time Lifetime unlock.
  • Is it a subscription? No. Loopart Pro is a one-time purchase tied to your Apple ID. Buy once, keep forever.
  • How do I restore my purchase? Open Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. Pro is tied to your Apple ID.
  • Do I need an account? No. Loopart has no accounts and no login. Your creations stay on your device until you export and share them.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Loopart is designed to be private by default. There are no accounts, and your patterns are created and stored on your device. This Privacy Policy explains the limited data processed by the app and by the third-party services used for ads and purchases.

Information You Provide

  • The pattern settings you choose (pattern type, parameters, palette, and similar creative options).
  • Exported videos and GIFs are saved where you choose to send them — for example, the Photos app or a share destination you select.

We do not operate an account system and we do not upload your creations to our own servers.

AdMob may process identifiers, advertising data, product interaction data, diagnostics, and related data for advertising and measurement. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for model training. Data processed for advertising is handled by Google in accordance with its policies.

Data Retention and Deletion

Pattern settings and exports remain on your device until you delete them or remove the app. Removing the app deletes its on-device data.

Children's Privacy

Loopart is a general-audience creative tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
