Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your and your child's personal information.

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use KidGrow, we may collect the following information:

  • Child profile data: name (optional), date of birth, gender, and height measurements
  • Parent/guardian data: parental heights (used for genetic height prediction)
  • Usage data: habit logs, sleep records, meal and supplement entries
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, app version, and language preference
  • In-app purchase records processed by Apple

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate personalised growth predictions and health recommendations
  • Power the AI Coach feature (conversations are processed via OpenAI's API)
  • Process subscription payments (handled by RevenueCat and Apple)
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Third-Party Services

KidGrow uses the following third-party services, each subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Apple (In-App Purchases & iCloud) — manages subscription billing and authentication
  • RevenueCat — processes and verifies subscription status; may collect purchase metadata
  • OpenAI — powers the AI Coach chat feature; conversation data is transmitted to OpenAI's API and subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy

We do not sell your data to any third party, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored on your device and, where cloud sync is enabled, in Apple's iCloud infrastructure. We retain data associated with your account for as long as you have the app installed. If you delete the app or request data deletion, your information will be removed within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy

KidGrow is designed to be used by parents and guardians on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly allow children to create accounts directly. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child without appropriate parental consent, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion — request that your data be erased ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — we do not sell personal data, so no opt-out is required under CCPA

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at pals_green.1m@icloud.com.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Your continued use of KidGrow after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at:
pals_green.1m@icloud.com
