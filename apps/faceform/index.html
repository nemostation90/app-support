Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

FaceForm is designed as a local-first, private wellness app. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed by the app and by the third-party services used for ads and purchases.

Information You Provide

  • Progress photos you choose to capture or import into the journal.
  • Routine completion logs, streaks, goals, reminder times, and notes you enter.

This content is stored locally on your device. We do not operate an account system and we do not upload your photos or notes to our own server.

Camera and Photo Library

The app asks for camera or photo library access only when you choose to take or import a journal photo. You can manage these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

In-App Purchases

The Lifetime Unlock is a one-time, non-consumable purchase processed by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may process payment, purchase history, and account information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policy. We never see your payment details.

Advertising

The free version shows Google AdMob banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may process identifiers, advertising data, product interaction data, diagnostics, and related data for advertising and measurement. The app requests App Tracking Transparency permission before any tracking; you can decline and still use every feature. Purchasing the Lifetime Unlock removes all ads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal photos or notes. We do not use your photos for medical diagnosis, model training, or advertising.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your in-app photos, logs, streaks, and notes remain on your device until you delete them or remove the app. You can delete individual journal entries at any time from within the app.

Wellness Disclaimer

FaceForm provides general wellness and relaxation routines for personal use. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Stop any exercise that causes pain or discomfort and consult a qualified healthcare professional with questions about a medical condition.
