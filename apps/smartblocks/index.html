Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Smart Blocks.

Drag the block pieces from the tray at the bottom onto the grid. Your goal is to fill the highlighted target shape exactly — no gaps, no overlaps. Every piece must be used. Once the shape is complete, you advance to the next level.

Tap the lightbulb button in the action bar. The hint places one piece for you and uses your free hint for that level. Free users get one hint per level. Premium users get unlimited hints. You can also watch a rewarded ad to unlock a hint if you've used your free one.

The Daily Challenge gives you a new timed puzzle every day. All players worldwide get the same puzzle so you can compare scores on the leaderboard. Complete it before midnight to keep your streak alive. Tap the "Daily Challenge" button on the main game screen to start.

Smart Blocks syncs your progress via iCloud. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID on your device and that iCloud Drive is enabled. Your highest level, score, and in-progress game are restored automatically when you reinstall or switch devices.

Premium removes all ads and unlocks unlimited undos and unlimited hints. Available as a monthly subscription, a yearly subscription (best value), or a one-time Lifetime purchase (€15.99 — pay once, own forever). Manage your subscription anytime in the App Store.

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel: open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → tap Smart Blocks → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Your Premium access continues until then.

Try dragging the piece slowly over the grid — a ghost preview appears when the piece is near a valid position. Make sure you're dragging from the piece itself in the tray, not the edges of the screen. If the issue persists, force-quit and reopen the app.

The undo button removes the last piece you placed and returns it to the tray. Free users get 2 undos per game. Premium users get unlimited undos. You can also watch a rewarded ad to unlock additional undos when you've used your free ones.

Your privacy matters. Here is exactly what we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: March 2025

When you use Smart Blocks, we may collect the following:

  • Game progress data: current level, best score, in-progress game state, daily challenge completion, and streak — stored locally and synced via iCloud.
